Privacy Policy

What we collect on this website, and why.

This page is a plain-language draft, not legal advice. Last updated: July 7, 2026.

Who we are

Sina's Roofing & Repair, 41 Crosby Ave, Richmond Hill, ON L4C 0B2. Reach us by phone at (647) 769-2769 for any question about this policy or your information.

Contact form

The forms on this site (Home and Contact pages) ask for your name, phone number, an optional email, and a message about your roofing project. Online submission is being set up — right now, submitting the form does not send your details anywhere; it stays in your browser and is discarded. Once online submissions are live, form details will be emailed to us through a third-party form service (Formspree), and this policy will be updated to name that processor and link to its privacy policy.

Map

Our Contact page can show a Google Map of our location. The map only loads if you click "Load map" — until then, no map content or Google cookies are loaded. Once loaded, Google may collect information per its own privacy policy: policies.google.com/privacy.

Analytics and cookies

This site does not currently use any website analytics or tracking tool, and does not set its own cookies. If that changes, this policy will be updated to name the tool.

Your choices

You can browse this site without submitting the contact form or loading the map. To ask what information we have about you, or to request it be deleted, call us at (647) 769-2769.
