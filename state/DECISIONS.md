### Phase 2 design rationale — "Angled Trust"
- Direction: "Angled Trust" — bold, no-nonsense trade-contractor site that reads as fast-response and reliable, not luxury/artistic. Niche playbook: hvac.md (closest match for trades/home-services; adapted for roofing-specific proof: before/after repair photos, storm/leak urgency).
- Mood source: client's own Vibe section (Bold, Clean, Dependable, Local, Strong, No-nonsense) — treated as the mood brief since Creative's Mood line was left blank.
- Color intent: light-dominant (≈60% white/#FFFFFF + light gray #F3F5F7), navy/charcoal contrast blocks (≈25%: #003B73, #002A55, #1C1C1E, #2F3033) for trust sections (about, footer, stats), orange #F05A28/#C93A1A reserved (≈15%) for CTAs, emergency callouts, active states only. Color mode: light base. Full hex palette already given verbatim by client — design-tokens expands it, does not reinterpret it.
- Type pairing intent: heavy/bold grotesk sans for display headlines (large, tight tracking, "Large bold headlines" per Vibe) paired with a clean neutral sans for body copy — same-family or close-sibling pairing, no serif, no script (avoids "luxury/artistic" the client explicitly rejected).
- Distinctive element (ONE): sharp angled accents inspired by the logo — diagonal-cut section dividers / clipped corners on cards and CTA bands, used sparingly as the site's signature geometry (not on every section).
- Imagery style: photo-real, high-contrast documentary job-site photography — roofs, shingles, before/after repair pairs; no illustration, no stock-glossy staging. Photos policy unconfirmed in Overrides (logged in QUESTIONS.md); imagery style decision here is independent of that and holds regardless.
- Layout personality: clean rectangular sections, strong grid, generous whitespace on light sections, bold full-bleed navy/charcoal blocks for contrast — zero soft/organic shapes, zero cleverness (per hvac playbook "avoid ambiguous CTAs / cleverness").
- Motion level: subtle (scroll fades/slide-ins, snappy sharp-edged transitions echoing the angled-accent motif) — matches "no-nonsense" mood and hvac playbook's "avoid animation-heavy pages"; final Stack animation flag decided at Phase 5 (Overrides left it unset → propose subtle/css-only).
- Must-have sections carried from playbook: phone visible in header everywhere, emergency/24-7 badge (confirmed true), service-area line, trust badges/certifications slot (licenses unconfirmed — placeholder), testimonials, short quote/contact form.
- Logo: client/assets-intake/logo.png present — angled accent motif and navy palette should read as literally sourced from it.
