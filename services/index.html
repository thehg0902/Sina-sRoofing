Shingle Roof Repair

Missing, curling, or cracked shingles let water into your home. We repair damaged sections or replace the whole roof, matching your existing shingle style and color where we can. If you're seeing granules in the gutter or daylight in the attic, it's time for a look.

Flat Roof Repair

Flat roofs fail differently than shingle roofs — ponding water, membrane cracks, and seam leaks are the usual suspects. We find the source of the leak and fix it properly, not just patch the symptom.

Gutter Repair & Replacement

Clogged or damaged gutters send water straight down your foundation instead of away from it. We repair sagging, leaking, or disconnected gutters, and replace sections that are too far gone.

General Roof Repairs

Storm damage, wind-lifted shingles, ice damming, general wear — if your roof has a problem, we've likely fixed it before. We inspect, diagnose, and repair, with a clear explanation of what happened and what it'll take to fix.

Skylight Repair

A leaking skylight is almost always a flashing or seal problem, not the glass itself. We reseal, refit, or replace skylight flashing so your natural light stops coming with a side of water damage.
